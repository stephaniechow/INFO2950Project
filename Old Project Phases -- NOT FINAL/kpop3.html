CHART

  • Notes*
    • Artists songs which are currently in promotion may be included in the chart.
    • OST tracks are not included. Head over to Mnet.com to check out OST rankings.
      (Also displayed in a scrolling ticker at bottom of screen during broadcasts)
    • Triple Crown (#1 for three consecutive weeks) winning songs will be taken out of the running automatically
  • How is The M COUNTDOWN Chart Calculated?

    • Digital Music Sales Score

      45%
      A total weekly song ranking based on data from four, online, Korean music companies (Mnet, Melon, Genie, Bugs).

    • Album Sales Score

      15%
      A ranking which reflects weekly sales volume based on data from the Hanteo Chart.

    • Social Media Score

      20%
      YouTube M/V Views + M COUNTDOWN Live Clip Views

    • Global fan voting

      10%
      > Sum online voting results on domestic and international from M COUNTDOWN website (Mnet, Mwave) and Mnet JAPAN.

    • Broadcast Score

      10%
      Mnet Channel Broadcast score → MV, broadcast, etc...
